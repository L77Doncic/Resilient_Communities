  • 2025 Was Among the Three Warmest Years on Record 2025年位列有记录以来最热的三个年份之一

    2026.03.232026.03.23

    WMO says 2015-2025 were the hottest 11 years on record. For cities, that means stronger heat, heavier downpours, and more pressure on power, water, and health services. 世界气象组织指出，2015至2025年是有记录以来最热的11年。对城市而言，这意味着更强的高温、更急的强降雨，以及对供电、供水和医疗系统更大的压力。

    Source: WMO, State of the Global Climate 2025 来源：世界气象组织《2025年全球气候状况》
    Open source summary 点击展开来源摘要

    WMO's report released on March 23, 2026 says 2015-2025 were the hottest 11 years on record, with 2025 ranking second or third warmest. It highlights rising heat, heavy rainfall, tropical cyclones, ocean warming, and wider social disruption. 世界气象组织于2026年3月23日发布的报告指出，2015至2025年是有记录以来最热的11年，其中2025年排名第二或第三。报告特别强调了高温增强、强降雨、热带气旋、海洋持续升温以及更广泛的社会影响。

    wmo.int/publication-series/state-of-global-climate/state-of-global-climate-2025

  • Forecasts and Warnings Work Best Before Streets Fill with Water 预报和预警的价值，体现在积水出现之前

    2025.10.242025.10.24

    WMO says earlier warnings, stronger forecasts, and better data sharing help communities act before heavy rain, heat, and storms escalate into transport disruption, safety risks, and higher losses. 世界气象组织强调，更早的预警、更可靠的预报和更顺畅的数据共享，能帮助社区在强降雨、高温和风暴升级为交通中断、安全风险与更大损失之前采取行动。

    Source: WMO on AI for forecasts and warnings 来源：世界气象组织关于预报与预警能力建设
    Open source summary 点击展开来源摘要

    In its 24 October 2025 release, WMO said AI-supported forecasting should strengthen early warning systems, not replace them. The practical point for communities is simple: better forecasts and earlier warnings create more time to protect people, transport, and daily services. 世界气象组织在2025年10月24日的发布中指出，AI 应当强化而不是取代现有预报体系。对社区而言，关键在于：更准确的预报和更早的预警，意味着能为人员安全、交通组织和日常服务争取更多准备时间。

    public.wmo.int/news/media-centre/world-meteorological-congress-endorses-actions-promote-ai-forecasts-and-warnings

  • Adaptation Works Best When It Starts in Daily Life 气候适应最有效的起点，往往就在日常生活里

    2026.02.102026.02.10

    UNEP's 2025 results show that adaptation is local: rooftop rainwater capture, stronger homes, and restored ecosystems all help communities cope with droughts, storms, and rising seas. 联合国环境规划署2025年的实践表明，气候适应首先发生在社区尺度：屋顶蓄水、更稳固的住房和生态修复，都能帮助人们应对干旱、风暴和海平面上升。

    Source: UNEP Annual Report 2025 来源：联合国环境规划署《2025年度报告》
    Open source summary 点击展开来源摘要

    UNEP's 2025 reporting shows adaptation working through local measures: rooftop rainwater capture in drought-hit areas, stronger homes against storms, and ecosystem restoration that helps communities reduce climate risk over time. 联合国环境规划署2025年的报告显示，气候适应通过本地化措施真正发挥作用，例如干旱地区的屋顶蓄水、更能抵御风暴的住房，以及帮助社区长期降低气候风险的生态修复。

    unep.org/annualreport/index.php/2025/stories/adapting-climate-change

FAQ 常见问题

  • Q 什么是韧性社区？
    A

    A resilient community can withstand and recover from disasters.

    韧性社区指能够抵御灾害冲击并在较短时间内恢复运转的社区。

  • Q 72小时应急包里有什么？
    A

    Water, food, flashlight, power bank, first-aid items, and key documents.

    建议包含：饮用水、食物、手电、充电宝、基础药品/急救用品与重要证件复印件等。
